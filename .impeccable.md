# Design Context — Inmersiva Grupo Inmobiliario

## Product
Real estate platform for the Peruvian market. Two audiences:
1. **Compradores/arrendatarios**: Browse property listings, save favorites, contact brokers
2. **Corredores (brokers)**: Manage listings, CRM, social media publishing

URL: inmobiliariainmersiva.com

## Brand
- **Name**: Inmersiva Grupo Inmobiliario
- **Slogan**: "Recorre hoy, vive mañana"
- **Personality**: Seria, moderna y aspiracional — professional without being cold, aspirational without being pretentious
- **Primary**: Navy `#1B2A4A` (oklch equivalent: oklch(23% 0.06 245))
- **Accent**: Gold `#C9A84C` (oklch equivalent: oklch(72% 0.12 80))
- **Market**: Peru

## Portal (Public)
- **Vision**: Social-media-style vertical property cards. Like scrolling an Instagram/TikTok feed but for real estate. Each card is 9:16 ratio showing the property's vertical marketing image with key details overlaid at the bottom.
- **Theme**: Light background — buyers browse during the day on mobile
- **Interaction**: Cards in a masonry/grid layout with filters at top. Click → detailed property view
- **Reference feel**: Urbania.pe listing density, but cards are VERTICAL (portrait) not horizontal landscape
- **Anti-reference**: Nothing that looks like a generic property portal, cheap, or corporate boilerplate

## Dashboards (Admin & Corredor)
- **Theme**: Dark navy — professional workspace used during the day by brokers
- **Feel**: Focused, efficient, information-dense without being cluttered

## Typography Direction
- **Brand words**: Aspiracional · Profesional · Vivo
- **Approach**: Pair a refined Latin display font for headings (something with character, not Swiss grotesque) with a clean humanist sans for body
- **Avoid all reflex fonts**: No Inter, DM Sans, Plus Jakarta Sans, Fraunces, etc.

## Layout Principles
- Portal cards: strict 9:16 ratio, image fills card, text overlaid on dark gradient at bottom
- Max content width: 1280px centered
- Mobile-first: cards stack to single column on small screens, 3 columns on desktop
- Filters: horizontal strip above the card grid, compact

## Motion
- Card hover: subtle lift (translateY -4px) + shadow deepens
- Favorites heart: scale pulse on toggle
- No bounce, no elastic easing

## Key Constraints
- All UI in Spanish
- FastAPI + Jinja2 templates (server-rendered HTML)
- SQLite locally, PostgreSQL in production
- Must work without JavaScript frameworks (vanilla JS only)
- Tailwind CDN already used in portal.html
